Map Shape 'Practically Confirms' At Least Nine Lost Bin Lorries, Experts Claim

A solemn breakdown of the latest imaginary map chatter suggests the city will once again be full of places for people to get mildly confused in.

By By M. T. Punctual Wednesday, June 10, 2026 4 min read

A completely invented wave of map talk has led to an important conclusion: the roads in GTA VI will almost certainly go somewhere.

Our panel of beige-jacketed experts studied a variety of pixel blobs, coffee rings and one particularly optimistic shadow, then declared the coastline to be 'very coastal indeed'.

One analyst pointed to a suspiciously bendy road and said it was 'clearly a narrative route for a stolen hot dog van'. Another insisted the wetlands prove the game will contain 'at least three heroic swamps and a fourth, secret swamp'.

The report also notes that any city with this many roads is likely to feature drivers, pedestrians, and a number of bins that will be relocated against their will.

Fans have taken the findings seriously, which is exactly the sort of thing fans are known for in these circumstances.
