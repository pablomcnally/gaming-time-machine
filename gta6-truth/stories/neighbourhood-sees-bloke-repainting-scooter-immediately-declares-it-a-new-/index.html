Neighbourhood Sees Bloke Repainting Scooter, Immediately Declares It A New Faction, Obviously

A weekend touch-up on a scooter has prompted rumours of rival clubs, coded colours and at least one regrettable soundtrack.

By By Brenda Nettle Monday, June 22, 2026 2 min read

A man on a side street spent the afternoon repainting a battered scooter while being watched with the sort of intensity usually reserved for coronations.

Within minutes, speculation spread that the fresh paint must signal a hidden gang, an allegiance switch, or a very committed errand.

Several residents were heard discussing ‘the red stripe meaning trouble’ despite the stripe being, by all accounts, mostly a stripe.

A passing auntie said the whole thing looked less like a criminal underworld and more like ‘a fellow trying to make the machine less embarrassing’.

By dusk, the scooter was still just a scooter, though now with a considerable amount of backstory.
