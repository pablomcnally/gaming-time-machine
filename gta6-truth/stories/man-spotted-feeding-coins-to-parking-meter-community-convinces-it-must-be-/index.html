Man Spotted Feeding Coins To Parking Meter, Community Convinces It Must Be A New Side Quest

A local parking meter has enjoyed unexpected fame after a gentleman gave it several coins and a nod, which authorities in internet circles call ‘immersion’.

By N. Barlow Thursday, June 18, 2026 3 min read

Residents of Vice City report a small but important incident: one man was seen placing money into a parking meter while muttering, “There you go, mate.” The internet has, naturally, decided this is not payment but a cryptic mission trigger.

Within minutes, speculation bloomed into a full theory orchard. Some say the meter is a disguised contact point. Others believe it unlocks a hidden minigame involving patience, regret, and parallel parking.

The meter itself has remained unavailable for comment, though it is understood to have accepted the coins with the calm dignity of a machine that has seen things.
